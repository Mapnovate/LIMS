    Data

    Land administration provides documentation on people to land relationships. It is an instrument for the implementation of land policies â€” part of the governmental policy on environmental sustainability, economic development, disaster management, social justice and equity and political stability. Land administration provides legal security (protection of all land rights), access to credit (collateral for mortgage or micro credit), spatial planning, land tax and resource management (mining, forestry, and nature).

    The data in this LADM system is in form of layers (spatial data) and also attribute data.The data is located on a central database that is being accessed by systems from the server.

    The data handled in the system includes;

    • Parcel Data
    • Landuse Data
    • Landcover Data
    • Rivers Data
    • Riperian Data
    • Basemaps
    • LandScape Data
