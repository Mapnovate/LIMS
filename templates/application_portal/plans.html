    Introduction

    A group of GIS proffessionals and land administration professionals initiated the development of the pilot land administration model system that facilitates the quick and efficient set-up of land processes such as search, registrattion and change of use/lease.Just like social issues benefit from proper land administration,land administration systems themselves benefit from proper data standards.

    Real impact is already visible in open source software development for land administration. Many countries still lack the modelling expertise to set up land administration systems.This systems goes ahead to provide a faster and quick platform for applications of processes and also data integration between entities in the land administration sector.

    Data

    Land administration provides documentation on people to land relationships. It is an instrument for the implementation of land policies — part of the governmental policy on environmental sustainability, economic development, disaster management, social justice and equity and political stability. Land administration provides legal security (protection of all land rights), access to credit (collateral for mortgage or micro credit), spatial planning, land tax and resource management (mining, forestry, and nature).
